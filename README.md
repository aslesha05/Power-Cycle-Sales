Power Cycle Sales Dashboard | Power BI
📌 Project Overview

The Power Cycle Sales Dashboard is an interactive Power BI report developed to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, product performance, and regional sales trends. The dashboard enables users to monitor key business metrics, identify top-performing products and regions, and make data-driven decisions through interactive visualizations and filters.

🎯 Objectives
Analyze overall sales performance.
Monitor key business KPIs.
Identify top-performing products and categories.
Compare sales across countries and cities.
Analyze monthly sales trends.
Provide interactive filtering for detailed business insights.
